Hello world!

I am Alex, I'm a PostDoc in mathematics at VU Amsterdam, working in the group of Sander Dahmen. I got my PhD from Boston University (working with Jennifer Balakrishnan), and before that I worked at TU Kaiserslautern (on the MPIR Library), and studied at the Universities of Cambridge and Warwick.
I am interested in many things, but mostly number theory and the formalization of mathematics currently.
